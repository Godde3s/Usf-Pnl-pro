    Keep-Alive Setup
  

  
Prevent Auto-Sleep

  
Render and Koyeb free tiers spin down after inactivity. Use UptimeRobot to keep them awake.

  

    

      

        
        UptimeRobot (Free)
      

      
Monitor your panel URL every 5 minutes with UptimeRobot's free tier (50 monitors). This prevents free-tier services from spinning down.

      

        1. Go to uptimerobot.com and sign up

        2. Click "Add New Monitor"

        3. Monitor Type: HTTP(s)

        4. URL: your panel URL (e.g., https://vless.onrender.com)

        5. Monitoring Interval: 5 minutes

        6. Save and enable
      

    

    

      

        
        Cron-job.org (Alternative)
      

      
A free cron service that sends HTTP requests on a schedule. No account needed for basic usage.

      

        1. Go to cron-job.org

        2. Create account and login

        3. Click "Create cronjob"

        4. Title: VLESS Keep-Alive

        5. URL: your deployed panel URL

        6. Schedule: Every 5 minutes

        7. Save
      

    

    

      

        
        Important Notes
      

      
HuggingFace, Railway, and Fly.io do NOT auto-sleep, so no keep-alive setup is needed for those platforms.

      

        

          For Render free tier, cold starts take ~30 seconds. For Koyeb free tier, cold starts take ~15 seconds. Setting up UptimeRobot eliminates this delay.
